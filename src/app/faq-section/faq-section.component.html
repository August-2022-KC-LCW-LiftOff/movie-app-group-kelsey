Frequently Asked Questions

Triggered is a movie trigger warning review app. We like to call it a safe space for movie lovers. Sometimes aspects of the movies we enjoy trigger us. This can significantly change a person’s movie watching experience (trust us, we know firsthand). To help people spend more time enjoying movies than being triggered by them, our app displays community reported triggers to warn others of possibly upsetting or triggering content.

With Triggered, you can search our movie database to view trigger warnings left by other movie watchers, so you can prepare yourself before seeing the movie. And when you create an account on our site, it makes you an authorized user and allows you to create trigger warning reviews for others.

Our site allows you to read reviews and learn about trigger warnings for classic, latest, and most popular movies. Though this comes in handy for a one-off search, you may discover a trigger that wasn’t previously reported. To create a trigger warning for others and save any movies for future reference, you must become an authorized user by creating an account.

To create a trigger warning review, first search for the desired movie in the search bar. Next, fill out the add trigger form underneath the movie’s description. Select two trigger warnings and leave a summary of the trigger.

Although we currently have 20 trigger categories to choose from, you may discover a new category, or have a suggestion for a subcategory to add.
