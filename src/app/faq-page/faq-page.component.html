How do I report a bug?

As we continuously work to make Triggered a better app, pesky bugs might pop up and disrupt your experience. If you spot a bug, be sure to smash it by emailing us at triggeredsupport@gmail.com.

 

How do I report a rude user?

At Triggered, we cherish our community and want to make sure it remains a safe space for all. If you encounter someone who isn't being a kind human being, report them to triggeredsupport@gmail.com, along with any examples you've seen.
