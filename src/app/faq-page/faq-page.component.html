About Triggered

Triggered is a movie trigger warning review app. We like to call it a safe space for movie lovers. People love movies because they offer an escape from the real world, but sometimes aspects of the movies we enjoy trigger us. This can change a person’s movie watching experience (trust us, we know firsthand). To help make sure spend more time enjoying a movie than being triggered by it, our app displays community reported triggers to warn others of possibly upsetting or triggering content.

With Triggered, you can search our movie database to view trigger warnings left by other movie watchers, so you can prepare yourself before seeing the movie. And when you create an account on our site, it makes you an authorized user and allows you to create trigger warning reviews for others.

Our site allows you to read reviews and learn about trigger warnings for classic, latest, and most popular movies. Though this comes in handy for a one-off search, you may discover a trigger that wasn’t previously reported. To create a trigger warning for others and save any movies for future reference, you must become an authorized user by creating an account.

We’re bonafide movie lovers, and know there’s a genre for everyone. So, we do our best to feature our community’s favorite genres. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Horror
  • Fantasy
  • Sci-Fi
  • Indie
  • International
  • Romance
  • Drama
  • Comedy
  • Action
  • Thriller
  • Musical
  • Historical
  • Biopic
  • Animation

General App Questions

To create a trigger warning review, first search for the desired movie in the search bar. Next, fill out the add trigger form underneath the movie’s description. Select two trigger warnings and leave a summary of the trigger. Your summary can include a time stamp or explanation of the scene to further inform other users. Once complete, click “add trigger tag,” to submit your review. Note: It may take up to 5 days for your review to display on the page.

Although we currently have 20 trigger categories to choose from, you may discover a new category, or have a suggestion for a subcategory to add. Thank you so much for your dedication to making Triggered a safe space for movie watchers! Please email us at triggeredsupport@gmail.com with the subject line: “Trigger category suggestion”

Unfortunately, our app currently does not support adding/removing items to and from the “my favorites” list. This feature is in the works, and we hope to have it running shortly!

Rating movie reviews is a feature our app currently does not support. We apologize for its absence, but this feature is in the works, and we hope to have it running in the near future!

There are several reasons why a movie doesn’t have many reviews: our community didn’t find the movie triggering, not people have viewed the movie, or simply, users haven’t created an account to leave a review. If the latter is the case, don’t let another person potentially get triggered! See a need, fill a need, and leave the review for others. You never know you can help.

At this time, only authorized users aka those who have created an account on the Triggered app can create a trigger warning review. If you aren’t interested in creating an account, never fear! You can still search our database of movies for trigger reviews created by others in the community.

Customer Service

Because we are a team of four, it may be up to three days before you see your trigger warning review. If it has been longer than three days, please email us a triggeredsupport@gmail.com and we’d be happy to investigate!
